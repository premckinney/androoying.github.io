About Me!

I'm a junior at Cherry Creek High School in Greenwood Village, Colorado. I am part of a loving family of four that includes my mother, my father, and my younger sister Anna.

In 2013, I co-founded the Denver Math Club, a non-profit organization aimed at fostering elementary and middle school mathematical minds.

I am a classical violinist studying under the direction of Professor Linda Wang at the University of Denver. Since my debut in 2011 with the Littleton Symphony Orchestra, I have performed across the United States, most recently at Carnegie Hall in June of 2015.

In my spare time, I enjoy swimming, tennis, basketball, and debate.
